Meet Us

Eric Kopelovich, MBA

Hello, my name is Eric, and I am the founder of Sunshine Bookkeeping.
I have a Masters Degree in Accounting from Amherst University, as well as a Masters in Business Administration from the University of South Florida (Go Bulls!).
In addition, I am a QuickBooks Pro certified specialist.
I have spent over half a decade working in the accounting departments for local businesses
in the area. I am now branching out to offer private bookkeeping and payroll services to other businesses in the Tampa area and beyond.

When I am not in office assisting clients, you can catch me exploring the latest in
Digital Technology. I also spend time mentoring aspiring Accounting professionals
who are beginning to enter the industry.

Proudly serving the Tampa Bay area community since 2022
